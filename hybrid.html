Hybrid Farming

AVN - Transforming Agriculture, Empowering Farmers

AVN's Hybrid Farming Concept

Revolutionizing Farming with AVN's Hybrid Farming Concept

AVN is dedicated to transforming the future of agriculture by integrating informal and rural farming communities through our innovative regenerative hybrid farming concept. By offering a 5-year contract, we enable farmers to engage in hybrid food production on 2 acres of their land, combining traditional practices with cutting-edge technology to create sustainable and productive farming systems.

AVN's Farmer Member Package

Empowering Farmers with Comprehensive Support

Our Farmer Members receive a standard package that includes a regenerative farm design, a recycled smartphone, and an IoT system setup on their land. We provide the first year for free, allowing ample time for training and preparation in hybrid farming. Additionally, between Year 1 and Year 3, our Farmer Members receive $2,000 worth of inputs, ensuring they have the necessary resources to succeed.

AVN's Impact

Breaking Barriers and Unlocking Opportunities

AVN transcends intersectional barriers, driving socio-economic growth opportunities across sub-Saharan Africa with a forward-thinking approach. By focusing on four key aspects, we create a strong foundation for our Farmer Members to thrive:

  • Develop interconnected networks of members in digitized hybrid regenerative farming
  • Upskill members in advanced circular economics and Climate Change adaptation
  • Invest in members and facilitate access to tech and grow-on-demand markets
  • Empower members to build their financial histories and creditworthiness in a digital economy

Key Elements of the Hybrid Farming System

The Pillars of AVN's Hybrid Farming System

Our hybrid farming system is built upon the following key elements, designed to foster a sustainable and successful agricultural future:

  • Digital Financial Identity
  • Micro-Insurance
  • Training & Upskilling
  • Digital Marketplace
  • Fair Pricing
  • Multi-cropping including Trees
  • Regenerative & Carbon Sequestration
  • Pre-identified Markets
  • Agri-inputs
